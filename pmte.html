About this course

The Digital Product Management Tools training is a 4-week program that adopts a practical hands-on approach in equipping you with the right product management softwares and skills in order to manage your entire product life cycle; from industry analysis tools to roadmapping and flowcharting software, user survey and analysis tools, project management tools to collaboration and team messaging tools.

Who is this course for?

This course is intended for you! As more organizations are digitally transforming and optimizing their businesses, the need for qualified individuals skilled at using product development tools continues to grow. These tools will help you:

  • Describe, manage, and track requirements

  • Focus on bite-sized problems that can be solved quickly

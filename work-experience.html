How it Works

You will join an agile scrum team on a live digital product which provides you the opportunity to get your hands dirty gaining hands-on training and practical work experience. Please note that you will be working on real products not simulation, therefore, this course is not intended for persons with no previous product development knowledge/experience. During each sprint, your team is expected to carry out a sprint review where you receive feedback from major stakeholders and potential recruiters. You will be working on products such as: AI products for healthcare, our LMS, building a responsive website for our career academy and so much more.
